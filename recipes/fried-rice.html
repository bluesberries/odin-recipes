Fried Rice Recipe

Description

A simple fried rice recipe.

Equipment

  • Frying pan
  • Spatula

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Day-old rice
  • 1-2 Eggs
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs in a bowl and mix with fork or chop sticks.
  2. Heat the frying pan with oil.
  3. Add the egg mixture and stir to keep the egg loose and separated.
  4. Add the rice and stir.
  5. Add soy sauce.
