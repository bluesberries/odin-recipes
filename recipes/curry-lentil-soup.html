Curry Lentil Soup Recipe

Description

A simple curry lentil soup recipe that will feed you for a week. I forgot where I got the original recipe.

Equipment

  • Dutch oven
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, peeled and diced
  • 7 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 3 pieces of garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp curry powder
  • 8 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 10 yukon potatoes, diced
  • 1 kale bunch, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in dutch oven over medium heat
  2. Add onion, carrots, and celery in the dutch oven. Cook until onions are translucent.
  3. Add curry powder. Cook until fragrent.
  4. Add vegetable broth or water. Wait until boil.
  5. Add potatoes and simmer until soft.
  6. Add kale and take off heat.
